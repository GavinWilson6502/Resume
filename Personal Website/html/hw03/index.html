Wing Cap, Inc.

About

Welcome to Wing Cap, Inc., a company dedicated to providing free aftermarket software to help you make your hardware your own, with the additional goal of allowing you to keep perfectly good systems usable even after their manufacturers cut official support.

Products

Wing Cap OS

Our primary product is Wing Cap OS, a free and open-source GNU/Linux distribution. It can be installed on a wide range of systems, including PCs, servers, and even mobile devices.

M Series Hardware

Since installing aftermarket software may not be an easy task for some, we offer the M Series line of hardware devices, optimized for compatibility with Wing Cap OS, with Wing Cap OS pre-installed.

Services

Consulting

Wing Cap OS can be a great choice for businesses that rely on legacy systems, as we are committed to providing continued, stable support and compatibility for all possible installation environments. In service of this, we offer paid consultation to help you set up and maintain your systems with our products.
