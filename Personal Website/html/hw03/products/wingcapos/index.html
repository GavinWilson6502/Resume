Wing Cap OS

Product Description

Wing Cap OS is a free, open-source GNU/Linux distribution made to help you make your hardware your own, with the additional goal of allowing you to keep perfectly good systems usable even after their manufacturers cut official support.

Wing Cap OS can be installed on a wide range of systems, including PCs, servers, and even mobile devices. It supports legacy platforms just as well as modern ones, making it a perfect choice for extending the longevity of aging or discontinued equipment.

FAQs

What use case is Wing Cap OS intended for?

Wing Cap OS is meant to be used in a wide variety of situations. Our most optimized versions are those for PC, server, mobile, and embedded systems, but the operating system can easily be adapted for other uses.

I can't get Wing Cap OS to work! What should I do?

We suggest looking into our consultation services for assistance. By reaching out to us, you not only allow us to find a solution for your particular situation, but you also enable us to provide more general solutions for others by making improvements to the OS itself.

Why should I use Wing Cap OS over other operating systems?

Wing Cap OS is designed to be compatible with a wide range of systems, and painless to replace proprietary software with. Other operating systems tend to prioritize some platforms at the expense of others; we make sure to give all kinds of platforms equal amounts of attention, and not play favorites.
